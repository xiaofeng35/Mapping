HydroFlask Production

Hydro Flasks are manufactured in China by Ecoway Houseware Ltd, located in the Guangdong District. The company that produces these bottles is called EcoWay Drinkware and produces stainless steel bottles for a number of companies. China is the world's largest stainless steel producing country. These factories use both domestic supply and imported raw materials to produce these water bottles.

Hydroflasks Distribution

The products packaged in cargo containers and shipped to Bends headquarters in Oregon. The final step is distributing the hydro flasks across the US. From headquarters to distribution centers.

Iron Extraction

Most of the world's iron is extracted from Australia which is most likely one of the ways Hydroflaks obtains its Iron.

Chromium Extraction

Almost all of the world's Chromium is extracted from both South Africa and the Philippines.

Nickle Extraction

The same applies to Nickle almost all is extracted from South Africa and the Philippines.
